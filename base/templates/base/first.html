EMORSE
EMOtional-driven Recommender System in E-learning environment

After the COVID-19 outbreak, the teaching method underwent radical changes, mainly with the transition to e-learning. Even after the end of the epidemic, it was found that e-learning remains a more comfortable way of learning, and its benefits cannot be denied. However, the traditional method of distance learning is detrimental to students, resulting in less interaction and poor concentration among learners. It is in this context that our Emorse project is developed. This project aims to design and develop an intelligent application based on the detection of students' emotions to improve the quality of online education.
